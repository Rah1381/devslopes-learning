What Does x squared Mean?

For any variable x , x squared or x 2 is a geometric notation referring to a number being multiplied by itself - otherwise described as being raised to the second power

This notation can also be represented by the equation x * x or x times x

x squared is represented by this symbol x 2

Here, x would be known as the base , and the 2 would be known as the exponent.

The whole expression would be referred to as a power.
